# WayFinder – Product Vision

## Vision

To become the most trusted platform for families to discover special-needs support services through authentic parent recommendations.

---

## Mission

Finding the right therapist or support centre should not depend on luck.

WayFinder simplifies this journey by connecting families with trusted providers that have been recommended by other parents.

---

## Problem Statement

Parents often spend weeks or months searching for the right therapist, educator, or intervention centre.

Information is scattered across:
- WhatsApp groups
- Facebook communities
- Personal referrals
- Google searches

There is no single trusted platform where families can discover verified providers.

---

## Our Solution

WayFinder brings together trusted recommendations into one searchable platform.

Parents can:

- Search providers
- Filter by service
- Filter by locality
- View provider information
- Recommend new providers
- Help other families

---

## Target Audience

### Primary Users

Parents and caregivers of children with developmental, learning, or special needs.

### Secondary Users

- Therapists
- Special educators
- Intervention centres
- NGOs
- Support groups

---

## Core Principles

- Community first
- Parent trust over advertisements
- Simple and accessible
- Mobile friendly
- Transparent information

---

## Success Metrics

### MVP

- 100 providers
- 50 parent recommendations
- 500 monthly users

### Version 1.0

- 500 providers
- 5,000 monthly users
- Coverage across Chennai

---

## Long-Term Vision

Expand beyond Chennai and become India's trusted directory for special-needs support services.
